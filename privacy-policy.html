Overview

BOOKISTUDIO ("we," "our," or "us") is a web design studio operated by Adrien Muhabuki. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide when you visit our website or submit a project inquiry through our contact or client intake forms.

By using our website or submitting any form, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not submit personal information through our site.

Plain English summary: We collect your contact details and business information solely to respond to your project inquiries and deliver our web design services. We do not sell your data. Ever.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily submit through our website forms. This includes:

Contact Form

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Business name
  • Project budget range
  • Project description (free-text message)

Client Intake Form

  • Full name and job title
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business name, industry, address (street, city, state, ZIP)
  • Business website URL and year founded
  • Business hours and social media profile URLs
  • Owner biography and business story (free-text)
  • Service descriptions and pricing (free-text)
  • Uploaded files including logos, business photos, and owner photos
  • Domain name preferences and design reference websites
  • Referral source (how you heard about us)
  • Special project requirements (free-text)

We do not collect payment or financial information through this website. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively for business purposes related to your inquiry and our service delivery:

  • To respond to your project inquiry and answer questions
  • To prepare quotes, proposals, and project scopes
  • To deliver contracted web design and development services
  • To communicate project updates, deadlines, and deliverables
  • To use uploaded assets (logos, photos, copy) to build your website as instructed
  • To improve our intake and onboarding process

We will not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails. If we ever send promotional communications, we will provide a clear unsubscribe option in every message.

03

How We Store Your Data

Form submissions are stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. Supabase servers are hosted on AWS infrastructure in the United States. Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type II and GDPR standards.

Email notifications of new inquiries are routed through Web3Forms, a form-handling service. Web3Forms processes your submission data transiently to deliver the notification email and does not retain your data beyond that delivery.

Uploaded files (photos, logos) submitted through the intake form are stored in Supabase Storage, a secure object storage service.

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for, or as required by applicable law. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days unless we are legally required to retain it.

04

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — Database and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Web3Forms — Form-to-email notification service (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — Web font delivery (font requests may be logged by Google)

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for their own marketing purposes. We only share your data with service providers who assist in operating our website or delivering our services, and only to the extent necessary.

05

Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies, analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics), or advertising pixels. We do not track your browsing behavior across other websites.

Your browser may store small technical items (such as session tokens) necessary for basic site functionality, but no persistent tracking cookies are set by us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal information ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to how we process your data in certain circumstances

Residents of California (CCPA), the European Union / European Economic Area (GDPR), and other jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws are entitled to these rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 09 below.

07

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all form submissions
  • Encrypted storage via Supabase (AES-256 at rest)
  • Row-level security policies restricting access to stored records
  • Password-protected admin access to stored inquiries

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
